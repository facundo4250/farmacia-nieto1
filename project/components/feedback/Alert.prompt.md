Inline banner for confirmations, reminders, and gentle warnings. Comes with built-in status icons.

```jsx
<Alert variant="success" title="¡Pedido recibido!">Te avisamos por WhatsApp cuando esté lista.</Alert>
<Alert variant="warning">Tu fórmula requiere receta médica vigente.</Alert>
```

Variants: `info`, `success`, `warning`, `danger`. Pass `icon` to override.
